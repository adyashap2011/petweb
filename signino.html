About Petriarchy

Petriarchy helps pet parents keep their pets happy and healthy through a line of at-home urine test products. Using the same diagnostic test strips that veterinarians use in their clinics and with results easily shareable, Petriarchy makes healthy living easier for pet families. With Petriarchy, a lifetime of love is only a scan away.
