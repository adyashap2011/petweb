About Petnostics

Petnostics helps pet parents keep their pets happy and healthy through a line of at-home urine test products. Using the same diagnostic test strips that veterinarians use in their clinics and with results easily shareable, Petnostics makes healthy living easier for pet families. With Petnostics, a lifetime of love is only a scan away.
